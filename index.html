Our Story

The Vantis Story

Vantis was born on a ski lift.

Not a metaphorical one — an actual, freezing-cold, slow-moving ski lift above Vail, Colorado. Our founder, a lifelong Coloradan and recovering mechanical engineer, was watching the mountains he loved disappear into smog from diesel busses idling in traffic below. The mountains that were supposed to clear his head were being choked by the very vehicles carrying people to enjoy them.

He didn't want an electric car that happened to be good for the environment. He wanted an electric car that happened to be the best vehicle on the road — in the snow, on the highway, in a parking lot, on a gravel road no GPS has ever heard of. A vehicle that earned its place next to the mountains.

That's what we built. That's what Vantis is.

No Compromises

Not in materials. Not in engineering. Not in the driving experience. We don't ship anything we'd be embarrassed to park at a trailhead — or a Fortune 500 headquarters.

Built Where It Matters

Designed in Denver. Every vehicle tested on Loveland Pass before it ever reaches a customer. If it doesn't work here, it doesn't work.

Transparent by Default

Range figures we can defend. Performance numbers we can prove. No marketing asterisks. No "up to" disclaimers buried in footnotes.

Responsible by Design

Zero emissions isn't a feature — it's the baseline. But sustainability isn't just about the drivetrain. It's in the reclaimed wood, the recycled materials, the packaging, the company we keep.

Built for the Driver

Not the algorithm. Not the segment. Not the safety regulator. The person behind the wheel. That person deserves something exceptional.

Technology

The Technology That Powers
Beyond Elevation

We didn't build an electric vehicle. We rebuilt what an electric vehicle can be. Vantis technology starts with a clean sheet — not a legacy platform retrofitted for batteries — and ends with a vehicle that sets new benchmarks for the industry.

01 — Platform

The Vantis E-Platform

Built from scratch. Built to last.

The Vantis Electric Platform is a purpose-built architecture — not a converted gas platform with batteries stuffed underneath. A low center of gravity. Structural battery integration. Optimized for both performance and range from day one.

  • Structural battery pack integrated into chassis
  • Liquid-cooled thermal management: -40°F to 130°F
  • OTA updates for powertrain, suspension, autonomy
  • V2-grid capability — power your home from your vehicle
02 — Battery

Battery Technology

More miles. Less waiting.

Next-generation lithium-iron-phosphate (LFP) chemistry with our proprietary energy density optimization. More range per dollar, faster charging, and a battery that holds 90%+ capacity at 200,000 miles.

  • 420-mile range (APEX) / 390-mile range (RIDGE)
  • 350kW DC fast charging: 20 min to 80% (APEX)
  • 11kW AC home charging — full charge overnight
  • Automatic battery preconditioning for fast charging
03 — Autonomy

Vantis Pilot

Your co-pilot. Not your replacement.

Vantis Pilot is our advanced driver assistance suite — designed to make long drives easier and city driving safer, without removing you from the equation. We believe the driver is the most important system in the vehicle.

  • Hands-free highway driving on mapped highways
  • Automated parking: parallel, perpendicular, valet
  • Intersection assist for complex urban environments
  • Night vision assist with thermal imaging
  • Standard on every Vantis vehicle
04 — Charging

Vantis Charging Network

Go further. Stop less.

Vantis owners get privileged access to the Vantis Charging Network — over 85,000 fast-charge plugs nationwide, including exclusive Vantis Ultra stations at mountain resort towns and highway corridors.

  • 85,000+ DC fast charge plugs via NACS alliance
  • Vantis Ultra stations at 200+ mountain & rural locations
  • Plug & Charge — just plug in, billing is automatic
  • Integrated trip planner with real-time optimization
05 — Performance

Performance Without Apology

Electric power. Earned feel.

We build sport-tuned vehicles. Not sporty-looking vehicles. Every Vantis model undergoes genuine performance tuning — on track, on mountain passes, on the roughest roads we can find.

  • Dual motor AWD on every model — all-wheel drive, all the time
  • Sport-tuned adaptive air suspension
  • Track Mode (RIDGE) — full system response unlocked
  • Brembo-performance brakes standard on RIDGE
